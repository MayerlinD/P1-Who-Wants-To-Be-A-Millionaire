Welcome to Millionaire,

the ultimate quiz competition! Your goal is to correctly answer a series of 15 questions. Sounds easy enough, right?

Not quite. With every question, the level of difficulty will increase and you will be racing against the clock to answer each question. Each question is worth a specific amount of money; the amounts are not cumulative. If at any time you answer incorrectly, the game is over and your winnings are reduced to $0 for tier-one questions, $1,000 for tier-two questions, and $32,000 for tier-three questions.
